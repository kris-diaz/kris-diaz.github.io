Corporate Clash

Corporate Clash is a completely free-to-play massively multiplayer online game designed to be a reimagined experience of Disney's Toontown Online.
Corporate Clash's website: https://corporateclash.net/ April 2022 - Present

Source code: https://github.com/kris-diaz/race-car-robot/

This was a class project four classmates and I completed for our Engineering Lab course at USF. We created an Arduino-based robot car that could be controlled via a phone.
